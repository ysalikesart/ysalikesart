Collage

This is a collage I made consisted with random images I could think of at the top of my head. The design is inspired by collage artworks I've searched on Google. This is like the average art piece but with inserted pop culture references such as the DeLorean from Back to the Future, Vector from Despicable Me, and the bike from E.T.: The Extraterrestrial. Enjoy!
